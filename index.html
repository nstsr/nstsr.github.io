Areas of Work

  • Thermal hydraulic analysis and CFD modelling of thermo-fluid systems and components
  • Prediction of potential failure of heat exchanger or steam generator tubes due to FIV (flow-induced vibration)
  • Assessment of potential damages on mechanical components (pumps, valves, tanks and piping) due to water/steam hammer, or Cavitation
  • Evaluation of performance of pumps and heat exchangers
  • Development of nuclear industry codes and standards for application of new technologies
  • Improvement of current nuclear safety regulatory system and analysis of reactor technology and safety issues
  • Standardization of small modular reactor (SMR) technologies and analysis of SMR safety issues
